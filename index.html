About Me

I am currently a Master of Science student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research interests include Computer Vision, Computational Photography, Machine Learning, and Virtual Reality.

I received the Bachelor of Science degree in the Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in December, 2015.

I am working on my thesis research in collaboration with Prof. Jia-Bin Huang at Virginia Tech under the supervision of Prof. Narendra Ahuja.
